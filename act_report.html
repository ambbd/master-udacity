WeRateDogs Data Wrangling¶

The dataset I have chosen to wrangle, analyze and visualize are from the WeRateDogs, Twitter account. I extracted the data from more than 5000+ tweets with years ranging from, 1900(manually default for empty values), 2015, 2016, and 2017 I manually and programmitacally brought in 3 files. I asessed the datasets by looking for any quality issues and tidying up the data where needed. By thoroughly asessing the data I brought in, I was able to determine what was valuable and provided key insights.

Insights¶

Numerator higher than denominator¶

I found out the numerator would most likely be ambiguous and non-mathematical based on the fact that there are skewed expressions where a numerator is higher than the denominator (i.e.)38/100, which was being used to rate the dog breeds in the text description. To avoid skewed results, I normalized the dataset.
